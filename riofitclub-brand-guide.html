Official brand system

Train where the city moves.

Rio Fit Club is a kinetic fitness, language, and social club for people who want Rio to feel less like a destination and more like a life they can enter.

Strategic Position

Not a gym. Not a class. A club in motion.

The identity should feel premium, social, athletic, and unmistakably Rio without copying local chain gyms. It avoids the obvious fitness language of dumbbells, flexed arms, shields, flames, bargain yellow, and sterile wellness minimalism. The brand signal is movement: wave, hill, track, rhythm, sweat, and language in the same system.

Logo

The Motion Crest

The mark combines a custom R with three Rio motion paths: coast, climb, and club rhythm. It should work as signage, app icon, shirt badge, sticker, social avatar, and event stamp.

Primary Horizontal

Default for web headers, entrance signage, partnership decks, ads, and official documents.

Core Mark

Use for small spaces, apparel, social icons, app tiles, wristbands, stickers, and class badges.

Stacked Lockup

Best for posters, square crops, event backdrops, profile graphics, and premium merch.

One-Color

Use for embroidery, engraving, stamps, monochrome print, and low-cost production.

Clearspace

Keep at least one motion-stroke width around the mark. On the wordmark, use the height of the word CLUB as the minimum margin.

Minimum Size

Digital mark: 32 px minimum. Horizontal logo: 180 px wide minimum. Embroidery mark: 20 mm minimum.

Misuse

Do not stretch, add drop shadows, place on busy low-contrast photos, recolor randomly, or combine with dumbbell/flame icons.

Color

Dark first. Electric second. Rio in flashes.

The green stays the conversion and recognition color. Cyan and coral are now supporting accents, used to keep the system from becoming one-note.

Display

ANTON

Use Anton for hero lines, posters, event titles, large pricing, and short commands. It gives Rio Fit Club the athletic punch that Lexend was missing.

Body

Manrope keeps the system human, readable, and modern.

Use Manrope for body copy, nav, forms, small labels, captions, course material, and interface text. The pairing feels sharper, more current, and more memorable than a single-font system.

Graphic System

Motion strokes are the brand device.

The logo strokes can expand into dividers, route lines, class labels, event cards, social overlays, progress bars, apparel marks, and animated transitions.

Signage

Use the horizontal logo in white/green on black or deep green. Add a cyan or coral edge stripe only when the environment can support color.

Apparel

Keep the mark large on backs, small on chest, and one-color for caps. Use coral sparingly for limited drops.

Digital

Use dark surfaces, sharp typography, stable 8 px radii, one green CTA per viewport, and cyan/coral only as signal accents.

Voice

Direct. Social. Alive.

Say

Train where the city moves. Come for the workout. Stay for the people. Rio access starts here.

Avoid

Generic motivation, empty luxury claims, sterile wellness copy, and overpromising transformation.

Tone

Confident but warm. Premium but not cold. Athletic but not aggressive. Brazilian energy without caricature.
